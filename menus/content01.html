Saddle Stitching Tutorial

Materials:

  • Paper that has been printed (folded in half)
  • Stapler
  • Scissors

Steps:

  1. Create a booklet by printing your pages and folding them in half. Make that the folded edge is level and smooth.
  2. With the folded edge facing up, place the booklet on a flat surface.
  3. With the open end of the stapler facing down, take it and set it in the middle of the booklet.
  4. Put a strong downward pressure on the stapler and staple the booklet's center. Ensure that every layer of paper is penetrated by the staple.
  5. Make sure the staple gets through all of the paper layers as you move the stapler to the top of the booklet and staple it.
  6. Make sure the staple gets through all of the paper layers as you move the stapler to the bottom of the booklet and staple it.
  7. If required, clip the booklet's extra edges with scissors.
  8. The booklet you saddle-stitched is now finished!
