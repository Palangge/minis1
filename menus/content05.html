Definetly not viral this is a step by step guide for Spiral Binding:

Materials:

  • Some love (my trademark secret ingredient, getting strongerer)
  • Paper (printed and cut to size)
  • Plastic or metal spiral coil
  • Spiral binding machine
  • Hole puncher
  • Cutting tool (scissors or paper trimmer)

Steps:

  1. Use a hole puncher to create evenly spaced holes along the edge of the pages. The number of holes will depend on the size of the spiral coil you're using, so check the coil's specifications for guidance.
  2. Once all the holes have been punched, insert the spiral coil into the first hole, twisting it in a clockwise direction until it reaches the end of the document.
  3. Use a spiral binding machine to crimp the ends of the coil together. Follow the machine's instructions for inserting the document and securing the coil.
  4. Trim the excess ends of the coil using a cutting tool. Be sure to cut them close to the crimped ends to prevent them from snagging on anything.
  5. Your spiral-bound document is now complete, hurrah!
Note:

Some spiral binding machines come with built-in hole punchers, so you can skip step 2 if you have one of those. Additionally, some spiral coils come pre-cut to specific sizes, so be sure to check the packaging before punching your holes.
